Mission Statement:

With the goal of empowering the sufferer who is in need of Judicial Remedy or an Alternate Dispute Resolution, we aim to convey this to one and all ; Ubi Jus Ibi Remedium which is a basic latin maxim meaning every wrong, the law provides a remedy.

At apnavakil.org Our motive is not to run after money or guide for litigation, our motive is simple – to get justice and solution as early as possible with minimal cost and shortest time span.

This is a kind of initiative to help those in need of justice or legal assistance.

It could be civil, criminal or anything that can be solved through law or mediation, conciliation or Arbitration.

We are just not another law firm, we believe in getting you a resolution, justice and peace.
